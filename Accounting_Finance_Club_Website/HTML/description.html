Accounting & Finance Game Description

Across the Accounting and Finance Club meetings, an ongoing interactive simulation game will be played. In the first round, you will each select a type of business that you will run throughout the game. Prior to each meeting, you will be required to complete a form for the next round, indicating some financial decisions regarding your business. At the following meeting, your business will be "in operation", some scenarios will occur (see example below) and some of your business's finanical accounts will be impacted as a result of the decisions made in the previous round. You will need to record the transactions that happened and update financial documents as necessary. During the last meeting, the player that has the greatest net income will be the winner.

Example Game Scenario

You must pay $500 rent for the month of November and pay for utilities
