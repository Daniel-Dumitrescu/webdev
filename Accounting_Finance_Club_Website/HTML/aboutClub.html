About Accounting & Finance Club

Accounting & Finance Club is the result of the merger of two former clubs at Colonel By, Accounting Club and Invest Club. The joint club, which began its first year of operation in 2019, exposes students to accounting concepts and has them apply unique accounting aspects in a creative manner through lessons and activities. The club also runs an accounting and finance simulation game to engage club members in applying ethical accounting procedure and planning strategic accounting scenarios.

Breakdown of a Typical Meeting

  • Introduce new lesson
  • Do a practice example
  • Play part of the game
  • Have fun!
