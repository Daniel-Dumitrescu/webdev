• A random word has been selected and you must uncover that word.
  • Each dash that appears on the screen represents a letter from that word.
  • Click on the button that corresponds with the letter you think is in the word.
  • If you are right, all of the occurrences of that letter will be revealed.
  • If you are wrong, part of your body will get hanged.
  • If you make 8 incorrect guess, you die!
